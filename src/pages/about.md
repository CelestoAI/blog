This blog is by the team at [Celesto AI](https://celesto.ai). We write about deploying AI agents safely — sandboxing, security, observability, and the infrastructure decisions that come with putting agents into production.

Most of what we publish falls into a few categories:

- **Agent security** — how to isolate agents, handle untrusted input, and avoid common mistakes
- **Infrastructure** — VMs, microVMs, containers, and what actually works for agent workloads
- **Tutorials** — step-by-step guides for building and deploying agents
- **Product updates** — new features and changes to the Celesto platform

## What is Celesto?

Celesto is a platform for deploying long-running AI agents and MCP servers. It handles the runtime infrastructure — sandboxing, tracing, and scaling — so you can focus on what your agent actually does.

If you want to learn more, check out the [docs](https://docs.celesto.ai) or the [main site](https://celesto.ai).

## Community posts

We occasionally publish posts from external authors. If you have something interesting to share about AI agents, security, or infrastructure, reach out — we'd love to hear from you.
